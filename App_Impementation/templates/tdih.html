The First World War lasted from July 1914 to November 11th, 1918 and was fought on three continents. Comprised of a myriad of events, battles, and skirmishes, a tragedy was occurring each and every day for those four-and-a-half years.

Usage Guide

To move through the timeline and onto the next event, click on the right-hand side of the timeline's current article. To move more freely, the events on the timeline themselves can also be clicked. Events on the timeline are organized by type and theatre to keep things a little more organized. Zoom buttons on the left-hand side of the timeline allow you to see a larger or smaller space of time. Below the zoom buttons you will find a back button. Be Careful! This back button will take you all of the way back to the title screen.
