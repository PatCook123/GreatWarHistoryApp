Further Info: Great War Nations

Usage Guide

WWI involved more than 100 nations from all corners of the globe. At the onset of the war, European colonialism dominated Africa and South Asia. As the war grew in scale, these colonies would be coerced to send millions of troops to fight in Europe. Many of these troops would hail from French Africa and British India.

To learn more about the major powers involved in the war, select a nation from the dropdown under "Select Nation" and click "More Info". If you'd like to learn about a different nation than initially selected, simply select another nation from the dropdown and click "More Info" again.
